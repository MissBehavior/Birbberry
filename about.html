Welcome to Iowa Parrot Rescue!



Discover a sanctuary where the chatter and vibrant colors of feathered friends fill the air! At Iowa Parrot Rescue, we are dedicated to providing a haven for parrots in need of care and compassion. Our founder, featured in the heartwarming scene of a gentle interaction with a stunning Military Macaw, embodies the spirit of our mission: to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome these intelligent and majestic birds.

Nestled within our spacious indoor facility, each bird finds a slice of paradise. From the intricate network of natural perches to the riot of colorful toys that decorate our space, we’ve created an environment that caters to the physical and psychological well-being of our avian residents. It's a place where the squawks of joy and flutters of activity never cease, as each parrot embarks on a journey towards a hopeful future.

Our commitment extends beyond rescue; we educate and advocate for the proper care and understanding of these complex creatures. With community support and the dedication of our volunteers, we strive to make a difference in the lives of parrots, one feathered friend at a time.

Join us in our quest to offer a second chance to these incredible birds. Whether you're looking to adopt, volunteer, or support our cause, you're always welcome at Iowa Parrot Rescue – where every parrot has a story and every helping hand creates a brighter future.

Come Visit Us at Iowa Parrot Rescue!



Step into a world where the love for birds is contagious! At Iowa Parrot Rescue, not only do we provide a sanctuary for parrots in need, but we also open our doors to bird enthusiasts and compassionate souls who wish to experience the joy these birds bring into our lives.

Our delightful indoor paradise, as shown with one of our happy visitors sharing a moment with a resplendent Hyacinth Macaw, invites you to witness the bond between humans and birds. This gentleman's smile is a testament to the fulfilling encounters that await you here.

Wearing his humor with pride, his t-shirt playfully reads, "Just a girl who loves doves and birds," symbolizing our inclusive and light-hearted environment. It's a place where laughter mingles with bird calls, and where everyone, regardless of age or background, can find a feathery friend to connect with.

Our array of educational talks, interactive sessions, and hands-on activities make every visit memorable. Whether you're a seasoned bird lover or new to the world of avian wonders, there's always something new to learn and experience.

So why wait? Spread your wings and come visit us at Iowa Parrot Rescue! It's not just a visit; it's an adventure that could lead to a lifetime of love and companionship with one of nature's most remarkable creatures.

Meet Our Feathered Youngsters at Iowa Parrot Rescue!



Get ready to have your heartstrings tugged by the charming antics of our baby parrots! At Iowa Parrot Rescue, we're not just a haven for adult parrots, but also a nurturing ground for the young ones finding their way in the world. The picture captures the adorable curiosity of a young African Grey parrot, engaging with a toy bell, an enchanting moment that exemplifies the playful and inquisitive nature of our avian juveniles.

With soft, grey plumage and eyes full of wonder, our baby parrots like this little one are sure to enchant you with their playful behaviors and eager learning. It's a joyous sight to see them interact with the world around them, developing their skills and personalities.

When you visit us, you'll have the opportunity to witness these early stages of life, where every new sound and texture is an adventure for our feathered friends. You'll see firsthand the care and dedication that goes into raising these intelligent birds, ensuring they grow up healthy and socialized.

Whether you're considering adopting a parrot or simply want to enjoy their youthful antics, our doors are open. Come experience the magic of new life and the tender beginnings of bird-human friendships at Iowa Parrot Rescue. It's a visit that promises to be as educational as it is heartwarming!
