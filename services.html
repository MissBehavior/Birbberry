Why Adopt from Iowa Parrot Rescue?



Welcome to Iowa Parrot Rescue, where feathered friends find their forever homes! Our adoption service is designed to match loving families with parrots in need of a caring and permanent home. By choosing to adopt from Iowa Parrot Rescue, you not only open your heart and home to a wonderful companion but also contribute to the well-being of these incredible birds.

Why Adopt from Iowa Parrot Rescue?

Compassionate Care: Our parrots receive expert care and attention from dedicated staff and volunteers. We prioritize the physical and emotional well-being of each bird, ensuring they are ready for their next chapter in a loving home.

Variety of Parrots: Whether you're looking for a talkative African Grey, a playful Conure, or a colorful Macaw, Iowa Parrot Rescue offers a diverse selection of parrot species. Explore our adoption gallery to meet the charming characters awaiting your visit.

Adoption Process: Our adoption process is designed to make sure both you and the parrot are a perfect match. We conduct thorough screenings and provide guidance on parrot care to ensure a smooth transition for your new feathered friend.

How to Adopt?



Visit our Rescue Center: Schedule a visit to Iowa Parrot Rescue to meet our parrots in person. Spend time interacting with them to discover the unique personality that might steal your heart.

Adoption Counseling: Our experienced staff will guide you through the adoption process, helping you choose the parrot that fits your lifestyle and preferences. We are here to answer any questions and provide valuable insights into parrot care. Home Visit: As part of our commitment to the well-being of our parrots, we may conduct a home visit to ensure a safe and suitable environment for your new feathered companion.

Adoption Day: Once approved, you'll have the joy of welcoming your new parrot into your home. We provide adoption packages with essential information, and our team is available for ongoing support as you and your parrot build a lasting bond.

By choosing Iowa Parrot Rescue for adoption, you are not just gaining a feathered friend; you are becoming part of a community dedicated to the welfare of parrots. Join us in providing loving homes for these intelligent and charismatic birds.

Ready to embark on a new adventure with a parrot companion? Contact Iowa Parrot Rescue today and start the journey towards a lifelong friendship!

Visit the Parrots



Discover the enchanting world of parrots by visiting Iowa Parrot Rescue! Our "Visit the Parrots" service invites you to experience the joy of interacting with these intelligent and charismatic birds in a safe and welcoming environment. Whether you're a seasoned parrot enthusiast or a curious first-time visitor, our rescue center offers a unique opportunity to connect with our feathered residents.

What to Expect During Your Visit:

Educational Experience: Immerse yourself in the fascinating world of parrots as our knowledgeable staff provides insights into different species, their behaviors, and the importance of conservation efforts. Learn about the unique characteristics that make each parrot special. Interactive Sessions: Get up close and personal with our resident parrots during interactive sessions. Enjoy the thrill of feeding, playing, and communicating with these charming birds, fostering a deeper appreciation for their individual personalities.

Guided Tours: Take a guided tour of our rescue center, where you'll have the chance to observe the various habitats, enrichment activities, and learn about our commitment to providing the best care for our parrots. Our team will be on hand to answer any questions and share heartwarming stories about the birds in our care.

Photo Opportunities: Capture unforgettable moments with our vibrant and colorful parrots. Share your experience with friends and family by taking photos with our feathered friends, creating lasting memories of your visit.
